Asian Legends Collection

Art Direction Symbols Spine 2D FX

A focused look at how we built the Asian Legends slot collection: readable symbols, Spine rigs tuned for mobile, and FX that stay performant across devices.

What we set out to do

Deliver a cohesive visual kit that balances premium feel with production-ready performance. Each symbol, UI element, and animation was scoped against strict budgets for mobile targets.

Symbol & UI pillars

  • Icon shapes stay legible at 72–96px in reels.
  • Gold trims and emissive accents held under controlled bloom.
  • UI hierarchy: bet, spin, and win states with clear glow ladders.

Animation & FX

  • Spine rigs: minimal bone counts, reusable timelines.
  • Hit / win FX: particle counts capped; glow gradients atlased.
  • Idle loops tuned to sub-1s cadence to keep reels lively.

Pipeline snapshot

01 · Style frames

Locked palette, trims, and bloom levels before moving to rigs.

02 · Rigs & atlases

Bone counts minimized; atlases planned at 2K with variant trims.

03 · FX & QA

Particle caps per tier; QA on low-end builds for overdraw and clarity.

Performance notes

  • Atlas sizes planned for 2K max; alpha where needed only.
  • Trimmed idle FX on low-end devices; preserved payoff beats.
  • Export presets shared with engineering for fast drop-ins.

Result: reels held 60 FPS on mid-tier Android test devices with FX enabled.

QA checklist we used

  • Symbols readable at 60% scale on 720p.
  • Glow ladders consistent across win/idle/spin states.
  • FX particle counts under budget in clustered wins.
  • Atlas memory within target on both HD and FHD.
